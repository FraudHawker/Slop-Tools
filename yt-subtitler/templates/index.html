yt-subtitler

Clip a portion of a YouTube video and burn in (translated) subtitles.

YouTube subs are near-instant when available. Whisper is the fallback / for videos without subs. First run per Whisper model downloads weights (small ≈ 500MB, medium ≈ 1.5GB).
