The point of showing these examples is honesty. These techniques work against basic Photoshop splicing, but they are largely ineffective against AI inpainting, generative fill, and diffusion-based editing. The high-signal checks (C2PA provenance, thumbnail mismatch, metadata integrity) are more reliable for modern image verification.
